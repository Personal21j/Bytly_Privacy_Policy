# Bytly Privacy Policy

*Effective Date: July 6, 2026*

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Bytly ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use and share it, and how we safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, Bytly (the "App"). It also describes your rights regarding your information.

Please read this policy carefully. We follow best practices and legal guidelines (e.g., GDPR, CCPA, COPPA) to protect your data. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with these terms, please do not access the App.

We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy and encourage you to review it periodically.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect information that is essential for the functionality of the App. This information is either provided directly by you or collected automatically. We do not collect advertising or device IDs (such as IDFA) and do not use third-party analytics or tracking cookies.

### A. Information You Provide to Us

- **Authentication & Account Data:** When you create an account, we collect your email address and a securely hashed password to manage your account. This is processed by our backend provider, Supabase. You also have the option to provide a full name and username. If you sign up using Google, we will collect your name, email address, and profile picture as provided by Google's authentication service.
- **User-Generated Content:** We collect the content you create and upload to the App, including:
  - Reviews: Ratings, remarks, recommendations, and any photos you attach.
  - Collections (Wishlists): The lists of restaurants you create, including their custom names and any icons you upload.

  All user-generated content is stored securely on our servers via Supabase.
- **Profile Information:** We store the optional personal information you provide, such as your full name and profile avatar, to personalize your experience. You can update or delete this information at any time in your profile settings.
- **Social and Friends Data:** We store your social connections within the App, including your friends list, friend requests (sent and received), and your list of blocked users, to enable the App's social features.
- **Feedback and Communication:** If you contact us for support or submit feedback, we collect the information you provide in your communication.

### B. Information Collected Automatically

- **Location Data:** With your explicit permission, we collect your device's real-time location (e.g., from GPS or IP address) only while you are actively using the App's map or navigation features. This data is used to show nearby restaurants and provide turn-by-turn directions via Google Maps Platform. We do not track your location in the background or store a history of your location. You can enable or disable location services at any time through your device's settings; however, location-dependent features will not function without it.
- **Usage and Technical Data:** We collect minimal technical data necessary for the app to function securely (for example, your IP address, device type, and app version for security and debugging). We do not tie this data to your personal identity unless necessary to resolve a technical issue.
- **Crash and Performance Logs:** The App includes foundational code for crash reporting. However, we do not currently use any third-party services to collect or analyze crash or performance logs. Our logging is restricted to development builds and is stripped from the production version you use. If we implement a crash reporting service in the future to improve stability, this policy will be updated.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate, maintain, and enhance the App's features and services. We will never use your personal data (like profile photos or reviews) for advertising purposes without your explicit consent.

- **App Functionality:** To create and manage your account, authenticate you, and ensure the App runs smoothly (e.g., to prevent fraud, perform customer support, and fix bugs).
- **Personalization and Social Features:** To display your profile, reviews, and collections to other users; enable social features like connecting with friends; and show you personalized content like your friends' recommendations.
- **Restaurant Search and Navigation:** To provide map-based services, including searching for nearby restaurants and providing turn-by-turn directions.
- **Communication:** To use your email for essential communications, such as account notices, password resets, and important technical alerts. You can opt out of any marketing emails at any time.
- **Service Improvement:** To use aggregated and anonymized data internally to analyze app performance, identify bugs, and plan new features.

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### A. No Sale of Personal Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share your data with advertising networks or data brokers.

### B. With Other Bytly Users

Your username, full name (if provided), and profile avatar are visible to other users. Your reviews and collections are visible to your friends and may be made public depending on future privacy settings.

### C. With Third-Party Service Providers

We share data only with trusted third-party services essential to provide the App's core functionality. These providers are bound by confidentiality and are not permitted to use your information for any purpose other than providing services to us.

- **Supabase:** Our backend infrastructure (database, authentication, and storage) is powered by Supabase. All your account information, user-generated content, and social data are stored securely in Supabase. We utilize Supabase's Row-Level Security (RLS) to ensure your data is only accessible by you and authorized users.
- **Google Maps Platform:** To power our maps, restaurant search, and navigation, we use the Google Places API and Google Directions API. When you use these features, data such as your location and search queries may be shared with Google. Your use of these features is subject to the [Google Maps/Google Earth Additional Terms of Service](https://maps.google.com/help/terms_maps.html) and [Google Privacy Policy](https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/).

### D. For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect our rights, or prevent fraud.

## 5. Data Security and Retention

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information. All data in transit is encrypted (HTTPS), and passwords are stored securely (hashed). Our backend on Supabase utilizes robust authentication and access controls. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will take steps to permanently delete your data from our active databases, subject to any legal obligations to retain it.

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your personal information. Depending on your location, you have specific legal rights.

- **Access and Update:** You can review, update, or remove your profile information at any time through the App's settings.
- **Permissions:** You can control the App's access to your device's location and camera/photo library through your mobile device's settings menu.
- **Account Deletion:** You can delete your entire account at any time from the settings. This action is irreversible. Under regulations like the EU's GDPR, you have a "right to erasure," and we will honor requests to delete your personal data without undue delay.
- **Legal Rights:** Depending on your location, you may have additional rights.
  - **GDPR (EU/EEA Users):** You have the right to access, correct, delete, and request a copy of your data (data portability), and to object to certain processing.
  - **CCPA (California Users):** You have the right to request information about the data we have collected about you.

  To make such a request, please contact us using the information below. We will comply with all valid rights requests in accordance with applicable law.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, in compliance with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take immediate steps to delete it.
